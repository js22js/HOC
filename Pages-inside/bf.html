Description

Brookefields is a shopping mall located on Brookebond Road (Krishnasamy Road) in Coimbatore, India. It was opened in May 2009. The mall has outlets from major clothing and apparel brands, a 06-screens multiplex cinema, and a food court serving multi-cuisine dishes.
