Description

Experience the joy of fine dining at our multi-cuisine restaurant, Tempt Asian located at the lobby level of Hotel Park Elanza. It is a melting pot of flavours from around the world, specialised in serving Asian food along with authentic Indian & global cuisines. An extensive menu with a wide variety of cuisines & top-shelf liquors to choose from, well-lit and decked up interiors, live kitchen and round the clock service makes it perfect to be the venue for any occasion starting from birthday parties to business meetings.
